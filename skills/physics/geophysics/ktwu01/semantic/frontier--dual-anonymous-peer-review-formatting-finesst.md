## Fact
NASA FINESST (F.5 in ROSES) requires Dual Anonymous Peer Review (DAPR) formatting — proposal text must not identify the FI, advisor, or institution until explicitly revealed in designated sections. Section A.1 (Earth Science Overview) Section 2.4 (Global Water and Energy Cycle) and Section 2.5 (Carbon Cycle and Ecosystems) define scope for plant-rock-water proposals. ECOHYD element uses "pulse event" framing. Point of contact: HQ-FINESST@mail.nasa.gov. Each proposed model addition must be explicitly mapped to a specific NASA satellite observable that validates it.
## Expiry Signal
ROSES is updated annually; verify DAPR requirements in current ROSES table before submission.
