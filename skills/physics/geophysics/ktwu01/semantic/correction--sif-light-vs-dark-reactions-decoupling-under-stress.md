## Fact
Solar-Induced Fluorescence (SIF) reflects the LIGHT reactions of photosynthesis, while Gross Primary Productivity (GPP) reflects the DARK reactions. Under stress (drought, heatwaves), these DECOUPLE — so using SIF as a direct proxy for GPP under stress conditions introduces systematic bias. Further uncertainties in OCO-2/3 SIF: (1) cloud contamination reducing valid retrievals, (2) sun-sensor geometry affecting apparent yield, (3) canopy structure effects on escape fraction. Use daily 0.05° gridded products with quality flags, and validate SIF-GPP relationships at flux tower sites before regional application.
## LLM Default Belief
LLMs often treat SIF and GPP as near-equivalent for remote sensing of productivity; they are not under stress conditions — this is precisely when vegetation modeling needs them most.
