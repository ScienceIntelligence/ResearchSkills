## Fact
The 2022 Hunga-Tonga volcanic eruption generated Lamb waves observable in the MLT (Mesosphere-Lower Thermosphere) region (80–100 km altitude) with TWO distinct propagation modes at ~320 m/s and ~250 m/s, reproducible in high-resolution WACCM-X simulations and observable at specific meteor radar stations. Continuous Wavelet Transform (CWT) of horizontal wind is the recommended visualization method for identifying these modes in both simulation and observation.
## Evidence
KW's work at NCAR (Han-Li Liu advising) presented at AGU 2023 and NASA DRIVE Science Center for Geospace Storms Workshop.
