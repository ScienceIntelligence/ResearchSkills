## Situation
In the user’s educational psychology thesis dataset, there were 371 collected questionnaires, and invalid responses were removed when completion time was under one minute or when answers were uniform across items.

## Action
The analysis text needed to describe exclusion of careless responses before formal statistical analysis.

## Outcome
The cleaned sample became the valid basis for descriptive statistics and hypothesis testing, rather than treating all 371 collected responses as equally valid.

## Lesson
For questionnaire-based educational psychology research, response-time and straight-lining checks are defensible validity filters and should be reported before inferential results.
