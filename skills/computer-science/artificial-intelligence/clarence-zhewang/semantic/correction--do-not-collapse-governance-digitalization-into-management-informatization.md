## Fact
In governance-oriented research, "digital governance transformation" should not be treated as interchangeable with "management informatization" or with a generic "digital transformation" label. The scientifically useful move is to distinguish these terms by their governing logic and transformation object, not just by technology intensity.

## Evidence
In the session, the requested revision explicitly replaced a technical reading with three concept-boundary comparisons: governance vs management, governance digitalization vs management informatization, and governance digital transformation vs generic digital transformation.

## LLM Default Belief
LLMs often flatten these terms into a continuum of "using more digital tools," which erases the distinction between administrative efficiency upgrades and changes in governance structure, coordination, and institutional logic.
