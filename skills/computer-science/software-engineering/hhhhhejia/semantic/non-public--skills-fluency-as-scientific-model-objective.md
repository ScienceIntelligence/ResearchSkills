## Fact
The Scientific Models pillar is not primarily about training models from scratch. It means continuing training from open base models so the model becomes fluent in using scientific skills, tools, and research protocols.

## Evidence
When asked what the Models pillar actually delivers, the scientist chose “continue training on open base models” and accepted skills-fluency as a core scientific model objective.

## Expiry Signal
Expires if the project pivots to closed proprietary models, from-scratch foundation model training, or treats models as independent of the skills registry.
